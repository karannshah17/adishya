About Aadishya

Engineering BTS of smart spaces - customised MEPF design solutions that balance performance, precision, and purpose.

The backbone of sustainable next-gen spaces

Aadishya is a specialized multidisciplinary design consultancy of MEPF (Mechanical, Electrical, Public Health, and Fire), offering comprehensive engineering solutions across sectors including infrastructure projects, residential townships and buildings, IT interiors and base build, healthcare, hospitality, data centres, retail, food industries, IT labs, sports facilities, solar power systems, pharmaceuticals.

We are a one stop design solutions company for plumbing, electrical, HVAC, automation, fire safety, solar systems, security systems adherence to Indian and international standards. Our process and team work closely with architects, structural designers, interior designers, projects managers, landscape consultants, clients, and the associates for seamless and on time project delivery with design intent.

Our core values include commitment to quality, time, and precision which has a proven track record. With our proactive mindset and technical expertise bridging client’s ideas into reality with meticulous, industry advanced, cost effective and desirable designs.

The strategic team at Aadishya is highly experienced to deliver error free, hight quality design solutions that meets all your needs as we always focus on client satisfaction.
